Successful Youth Development Program

The Calgary Curling Club recently concluded its month-long youth development program, aimed at introducing young participants to the sport of curling. Over 50 children aged 8-12 attended the program, learning basic techniques and fostering a love for the game.

New Ice Making Technology in Calgary Rinks

In a bid to enhance playing conditions and reduce environmental impact, several Calgary curling rinks have invested in cutting-edge ice making technology. The new systems promise more consistent ice surfaces, improving the overall curling experience for players across the city.

Regional Curling Championship in May

Excitement is building as Calgary prepares to host the Western Regional Curling Championship next month. Teams from Alberta, British Columbia, Saskatchewan, and Manitoba will compete for a chance to advance to the national level. Spectators are eagerly anticipating top-tier competition and thrilling matches at the event.
